Who We Are

Connecting Renters with Safe Spaces in Albay

ASpace (Aspacer) is a digital rental platform built exclusively for Albay, Philippines. We help students, workers, and families find safe, affordable, and verified dormitories, apartments, boarding houses, and bedspaces across Legazpi City, Daraga, Ligao, Tabaco, and nearby municipalities.

Our platform is equipped with security features that prevent scams and fraudulent listings — so every transaction is transparent, safe, and reliable.

Our Mission

To offer students and renters in Albay a safe, reliable, and user-friendly platform to discover verified dorms, apartments, and boarding houses, while promoting transparency, convenience, and security in every transaction.

Our Vision

To become Albay's leading trusted platform for finding safe and convenient rental spaces.

How ASpace Works

Find your perfect space in Albay in just 5 simple steps.

01

Search for a Space

Use our search bar and smart filters — including price, amenities and gender preference — to find available dormitories, apartments, boarding houses, and bedspaces across Albay.

02

Inquire with the Landlord

Message verified landlords directly through our secure in-platform messaging system. Ask about availability, house rules, and other details before committing.

Book an ocular visit at a time that works for you to personally inspect the property.

Book the Space

After your visit, reserve the space through ASpace. Our secure booking system ensures your reservation is protected and payments are handled safely.

Move In

Once confirmed and payments settled, you're ready to move into your new verified space. ASpace continues to support you throughout your stay.

Terms of Service

2. User Responsibilities

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Data We Collect

We collect personal information such as name, email, mobile number, and property details when you register or post a listing.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to facilitate connections between tenants and landlords, provide customer support, and improve our services.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted data storage and HTTPS connections.

4. Third-Party Sharing

ASpace does not sell your personal data. Data may be shared with law enforcement if legally required.

5. Your Rights

6. Cookies

Payment Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Subscription Fee

Landlords pay a monthly subscription fee of ₱299 to maintain active listings on ASpace.

2. Commission

ASpace charges a 5% service commission on confirmed bookings made through the platform.

3. Accepted Payment Methods

ASpace accepts: GCash, Maya (PayMaya), Bank Transfer (BDO, BPI, Metrobank, UnionBank), and Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard).

4. Refund Policy

Subscription fees are non-refundable. Booking deposits may be refunded within 48 hours, subject to landlord approval.

5. Payment Disputes

For payment concerns, contact Support@Aspace.com with your transaction reference number. We will respond within 3–5 business days.
